Rachel Clark Wiki and Family; Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Rachel was born in 1992 in Oswego, New York, United States. She is now 30 years of age and she celebrates her birthday in the month of May every year.

Talking about her family, Rachel is very close to her mother, Molly Manning Clark. Molly is hyped about her daughter’s appearance on Jeopardy!

Rachel’s mother Molly is looking forward to seeing her daughter compete on Jeopardy! (Source: Molly’s Facebook)

Moreover, Rachel has mentioned a detail about her family’s history on her Facebook account. As per her post on 5 October 2019, her great-great-grandparents left their village in Pomerania for the United States in around 1889.

Later between the 1890s and the late 1940s, they corresponded with the children and grandchildren of Rachel’s great-great-grandmother’s one sister who lived in Poland.

Rachel found those letters in 2013 and later she managed to connect with the writers’ surviving great-grandson on the family tree website.

With American nationality, Rachel has white ethnicity.

Rachel Clark Education Details

Rachel attended Stony Brook University in New York for her college education in 2010. She later earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science from there in 2014.

At Stony Brook University, Rachel graduated summa cum laude with departmental honors. Likewise, she was also a member of the Honors College.

Rachel graduated from Stony Brook University in 2014 (Source: Rachel’s Facebook)

Rachel was also a member of the Pi Sigma Alpha, Stony Brook College Democrats, and Think Magazine. Likewise, she was awarded the Michael Gramer Honors Thesis Award at Stony Brook in May 2014.

With the honor, Rachel also received a cash prize of $1500.

What Is Rachel Clark Job?

Rachel currently works at Rising Tide Interactive as a Director of Clint Strategy. She has been associated with the company for around three years and seven months.

Initially, Rachel joined the company as a Lead Strategist in October 2019. She held the position for two years and one month till October 2021.

After that, Rachel worked as the Associate Director of Client Strategy from October 2021 to April 2023. Since April 2023, she is involved in the company as a Director of Client Strategy.

Prior to that, Rachel worked at Veracity Media for two years and four months as a Senior Digital Strategist. She was previously a Communications Associate and Manager at Afterschool Alliance from January 2015 to June 2017.

Rachel also held the position of Associate at Convergence Targeted Communications. She worked there on contract from July 2014 to November 2014.

Initially, Rachel was an Intern for Congressman Bill Owens in the U.S. House of Representatives from May 2012 to August 2012. She then interned as a Field Fellow for three months from September 2012 to November 2012.

Afterward, Rachel worked as a Government Relations Intern at the Planned Parenthood Federation of America from June 2013 to August 2013.
