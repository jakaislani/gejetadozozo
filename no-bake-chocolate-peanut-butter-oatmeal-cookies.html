No Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies

When you don't have a lot of time and you need cookies FAST - these No Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies will save the day!

Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword chocolate peanut butter oatmeal cookies, no bake cookies Prep Time 15 minutes minutes Let Cool 30 minutes minutes Total Time 45 minutes minutes Servings 16 Calories 411kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup Karo light corn syrup
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3 cups quick oats
  • 1 cup peanut butter chips
  • 1/2 cup Reese's Pieces to sprinkle on top

Instructions

  • Combine sugar and corn syrup in a large saucepan.

  • Heat over medium-high heat until mixture reaches a full boil. Remove from heat.

  • Stir in chocolate chips and peanut butter until melted and fully combined.

  • Stir in oats. Let mixture cool for several minutes.

  • Fold in peanut butter chips.

  • Drop by rounded tablespoons onto parchment paper. Press several Reese's Pieces into the top of each cookie and let cool for 20-30 minutes.

  • Store in an airtight container.

  • Makes 3 dozen.
