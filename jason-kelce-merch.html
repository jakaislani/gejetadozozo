August 2024 · 4 minute read

Jason Kelce has certainly made waves with his pre-game fashion sense. In fact, he found his way to the headlines after going shirtless while cheering on the Kansas City Chiefs. So it comes as no surprise that the All-Pro center went sleeveless to announce his retirement. Kelce took to the podium to deliver a heart-wrenching 45-minute speech after spending 13 years with the NFL’s Philadelphia Eagles. He thanked a number of patrons, including his coaches, teammates, wife, parents, and brother, Travis Kelce, who was seen shedding tears during the press conference.

“There is no chance I would be here without the bond Travis and I share,” Jason Kelce said of his NFL star brother. “It made me stronger, tougher, smarter, and it taught me the values of cooperation, loyalty, patience, and understanding.” To commemorate the moment, Kelce wore a muscle tee with the Eagles logo and accessorized with a green rolex watch.
