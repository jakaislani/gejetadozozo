Nicole Arbour is a Canadian comedian, singer, choreographer, actress, and YouTuber who is best known for her appearance in films and TV shows such as The Rocker, You Might as Well Live, Silent But Deadly, Video on Trial, and Howie Do It. As a singer, she has released singles like Bang Bang and Fun Revolution. During the course of her career, she has been nominated for numerous awards such as the IAB Mixx Award and Shorty Award.
