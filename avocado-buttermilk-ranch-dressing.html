Avocado Buttermilk Ranch Dressing

Ranch just got a little bit more interesting.  I have yet to find something that this Avocado Buttermilk Ranch Dressing doesn’t taste good on!

Course Appetizer, Side Cuisine American Keyword avocado buttermilk ranch dressing, ranch dressing Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Refrigerate 30 minutes minutes Total Time 35 minutes minutes Servings 12 Calories 40kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 garlic clove 1 to 2
  • 1 green onion sliced
  • 2 tsp fresh lime juice
  • 1/3 cup cilantro
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill chopped - optional
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Blend all ingredients until smooth.

  • Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
