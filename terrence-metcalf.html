Terrence Metcalf Biography

Terrence Metcalf is an American former football player who was born and brought up in Clarksdale, Mississippi, United States of America. He is widely known for playing as a guard in the National Football League (NFL) for seven seasons during the early 2000s. He played for Chicago Bears from 2002 to 2008, Detroit Lions in 2009, and New Orleans Saints in 2010. He also played college football for the University of Mississippi (Ole Miss), and was chosen by Chicago Bears in the third round of the 2002 NFL Draft.
