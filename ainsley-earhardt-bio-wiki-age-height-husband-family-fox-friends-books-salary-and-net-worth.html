Ainsley Earhardt Biography

Ainsley Earhardt is an American television personality and author who was born in  Spartanburg, SC, United States Currently, she is the co-host of Fox & Friends which is a daily morning conservative news/talk program that airs live on Fox News Channel alongside Brian Kilmeade & Steve Doocy.

Previously, before joining FOX & Friends, Earhardt worked as a reporter for FNC where she covered many high-profile news stories. Recently, she sat-down for exclusive interviews with President Donald Trump, first lady Melania Trump, and Vice President Mike Pence.

    Ainsley Earhardt FOX News

    In addition to Earhardt’s role on FOX & Friends, she shows up across FNC’s on-request subscription-based streaming service FOX Nation as co-host of FOX & Friends After the Show Show, airing weekday mornings at 9 AM/ET. In December 2018, she introduced Ainsley’s Holiday Special on FOX Nation in which she featured uplifting stories for the holiday season and included charitable associations that she traveled across the country to highlight.

    Previously, she worked as a co-host of FNC’s early morning wake-up program, FOX & Friends FIRST (5-6 AM/ET), from 2013 until joining FOX & Friends as co-host in 2016.

    Before joining FOX & Friends First, she served as a reporter for FNC where she secured many high-profile news stories. She additionally reported on the Veterans Affairs hospital investigation in Phoenix, AZ, and the murder investigation involving the New England Patriot’s Aaron Hernandez. In May 2018, she gave on-location, live coverage of the royal wedding of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.

    Before joining FNC, she worked as an anchor for KENS-TV (CBS 5) in San Antonio, Texas and WLTX-TV (CBS 19) in Columbia, SC, where she was titled “Best Personality of the Year” in Columbia Metropolitan Magazine.
