Who Is Leigh-Ann Smith From Below Deck Season 10?

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Season 10 of Below Deck is set to premiere on Bravo this week with a brand new episode featuring the introduction of a new stew and the return of Captain Lee Rosbach. In the previous episode, Commander Sandy Yawn fired cook Alissa Humber.

Leigh-Ann Smith, her substitute, will join the boat before Captain Lee arrives in the forthcoming show. Deckhand Ben Willoughby and the visitor have dated in the past, so Leigh will recognize a familiar figure on the yacht. According to the show’s mid-season teaser, the two will share some private moments in the upcoming episodes.

Leigh-Ann Smith was born in Cape Town

Although Bravo has yet to release Leigh-Ann Smith’s formal bio, her Instagram handle reveals enough about her personality. Leigh is an adventure seeker who was born in Cape Town, South Africa. She describes herself as a “Motorsport Fanatic, Ice-Cream Addict, and Career Visionary” on her Instagram profile.

The majority of her web pictures show her competing in dirt bike events, as well as off-road motorbike and automobile rallies. She has worked in the yachting industry for over five years and is well-versed in it. She has worked on several superyachts, including “Q,” and was previously the head cook on another vessel.

Leigh is devoted to her family, which includes her father and two brothers. Her mother died in 2018. Her father, Captain Colin Smith, is a race car driver. Leigh two brothers are Wayne Smith (Africa Cinema Academy Award nominee) and Guy Smith. Leigh will make her television debut this week on Season 10 of Below Deck. She will work as a chef assistant to head chef Fraser Olender.

Below Deck season ten episode 14 date, location, and plot

“Cook Rachel prepares a memorable meal; the crew throws a 1980s party for the beauty queen guests; visitor LaQuish continues to annoy the interior team; Tony has a disagreement with Katie; and Ross is forced to confess his boat obsession.”
