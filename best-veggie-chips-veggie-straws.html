Best Veggie Chips and Veggie Straws [Taste Test]

May 2024 · 5 minute read

Veggie chips and veggie stick chips satisfy a very specific kind of craving. If you really want chips, they probably won’t cut it. And if you really want vegetables, they definitely won’t do the trick. They are their own snack category now. And there’s more than just Garden Veggie Straws out there. 

Before we talk about the taste and texture and enjoyment of veggie chips, let’s address the whole good-for-you thing: Are veggie chips healthy? Actual vegetables are, of course, much healthier. And, sadly, veggie chips often aren’t much different than potato chips. Most are made primarily of fried potato with some powdered vegetable thrown in for flavor and color. But some brands do advertise having less fat than regular potato chips. So, in short, no, you can’t replace actual carrot sticks with veggie stick chips. But that doesn’t mean you can’t enjoy them as their own kind of crispy, crunchy, tasty snack. 

So, with that out of the way, let’s talk about the search for the best veggie chips. We tasted 14 different flavors, shapes, and brands of veggie chips. We looked for crunch, seasoning, and actual veggie flavor. Because if there’s no veggie flavor, why bother? You might as well just eat an actual potato chip. 

We found seven delicious bagfuls that we all agreed were worth recommending—and one honorable mention. The Garden Veggie Straws Screamin’ Hot divided the group. Both managing editor Gwynedd Stuart and senior writer Jordan Myrick liked them, but I just think the flavor is all over the place. The spicy straws get points for genuine heat, but there’s no real flavor beyond that. Too one note for me, sadly. Still, if you want something crunchy and spicy, give them a try. 

Here are the seven best veggie chips we all think are worth adding to snacktime.

Best Low Sodium

Off the Eaten Path Chickpea Veggie CrispsTarget

At 150 mg of sodium per serving, these are the lowest sodium veggie chips on this list. But they still pack some good, snack-worthy flavor. There’s just a touch of salt and a lot of hearty legume flavor in these multi-colored chips. They’re made with chickpeas, rice, peas, and black beans—a departure from the usual mix of spinach and tomato. They’re also sturdy enough to dip!

Best Chip Substitute

Sensible Portions Garden Veggie Wavy Chips Sea SaltTarget

There’s not a ton of veggie flavor in these small, orecchiette-esque chips. But they’re extremely crunchy. “They taste more fried,” Jordan said. And that’s why I think Garden Veggie Chips are the best replacement for a classic potato chip if you’re looking for something marginally healthier. They boast 30% less fat than the “leading puffed snack,” as well as no artificial flavors or preservatives. It’s definitely not the same as swapping out chips for hummus and carrots, but it’s a step in that direction.

Credit: Merc / Target

Rating:

7/10

Best Veggie Flavor

Simple Truth Veggie ChipsRalphs

There’s no denying that these are veggie chips. They have so much veggie flavor. The tomato chip tastes like tomato. The spinach chip tastes like spinach. The chips are curvy and fun to eat and they are audibly crunchy. If you want the real veggie chip experience, buy a bagful of these.

Credit: Merc / Target

Rating:

7.5/10

Sporks

Best Bite-Sized

Good Health Veggie Chips Sea SaltVons

The bag would have you believe that these are pretty big chips, but don’t believe it—they are tiny, like silver dollars. At first, I was taken aback. But then I popped one in my mouth and ate it in one bite. Delightful. They have a good crunch and the colors have distinct flavors. They’re excellent veggie chips for mindless munching.

Credit: Merc / Vons

Rating:

8/10

Sporks

Best Classic Veggie Straws

Good Health Veggie Straws Sea SaltVons

I love the crunch on these veggie straws and appreciate that the person or robot who made them had a light hand with the salt. And this might seem like a small thing, but they don’t leave a coating on your tongue after you swallow like many fried, chip-esque things do. They’re very veggie-forward in flavor, though I’m hard pressed to identify a specific vegetable. Overall, these aren’t the best veggie chips but they are the best classic veggie straws we tried and I’d be happy to see them in the pantry.

Credit: Merc / Vons

Rating:

8/10

Sporks

Best Ranch Flavored

Sensible Portions Garden Veggie Straws Zesty RanchWalmart

If you love ranch, these are the ranch-dusted veggie straws of your dreams. “They say ‘zesty ranch’ and it is zesty ranch,” said Gwynedd, swooning. Ranch and vegetables go together, obviously, and so do ranch and veggie sticks chips. The creamy ranch complements the veggie flavors in these sticks, and Sensible Portions ironically went very heavy on the ranch—to everyone’s delight. 

Credit: Merc / Walmart

Rating:

9/10

Sporks

Best of the Best

Simple Truth Popped Root Veggie Crisps Sea SaltRalphs

Here’s how Simple Truth should be marketing these crisps: mini papadums! The cumin in these incredible veggie chips makes them taste like tiny, rice-based papadums. Incredible. I accept that these are not technically chips—they’re crisps. But they are just way too good to be anywhere but the top of this list. “These are outrageously good and I would be so happy if someone recommended them to me,” said Gwynedd. The packaging makes it seem like these would be bland mini rice cakes. Don’t believe it! They are chock full of flavor from the veggies (chickpeas, shallots, beets, and carrots—a fun, out-of-the-box mix) as well as the seasoning. Do yourself a favor and buy them immediately.

Credit: Merc / Ralphs

Rating:

10/10
